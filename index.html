Our Story

T.fly deDeejay is a pioneering electronic music artist who fuses African rhythms with cutting-edge EDM production. Signed to NLM Music Group Africa, a label founded in 2025 by Ailwei Maduna Wa Malada in honor of his mother, Ntampose Lettia Maduna. Together, we're pushing the boundaries of African electronic music and bringing its unique sound to the global stage.
