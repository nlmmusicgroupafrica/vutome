Our Mission

Sunday Sermons MusiQ is a podcast series dedicated to showcasing the authentic sound of electronic music producers. Each Sunday, a different artist takes control of the decks, presenting a mix composed entirely of their own productions. Hosted by I'M MusiQ, this series offers listeners an intimate journey through the creative minds of talented producers, highlighting their unique styles and production techniques. As part of NLM Music Group Africa, founded in 2025 by Ailwei Maduna Wa Malada in honor of his mother Ntampose Lettia Maduna, we're committed to elevating African electronic music and bringing its distinctive sounds to the global stage.
